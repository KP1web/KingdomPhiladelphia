Lead Pastor & Vision Bearer

Pastor Jamba Zira

Pastor Jamba Zira is the Lead Pastor and Vision Bearer of Kingdom Philadelphia Int'l — a Christ-centered ministry committed to proclaiming the Gospel, equipping believers, and advancing the Kingdom of God through brotherly love.

Called into ministry in 2018, his teachings are marked by clarity, spiritual depth, and relevance, helping believers grow in faith, maturity, and purpose. He carries a strong burden for soul-winning, discipleship, and the empowerment of the Church through the Holy Spirit.

He is happily married to Luba Zira Kumanda and blessed with three beautiful children. Prior to ministry, he practiced law in Nigeria for several years and is a graduate of Kenneth E. Hagin's Rhema Bible Training Center.

Associate Pastor

Pastor Keturah Johnson

Pastor Keturah Johnson is from Kaduna State, Nigeria. She holds a BSc in Entrepreneurial and Business Management and brings both academic rigor and a genuine pastoral heart to her ministry.

She is a graduate of Kenneth Hagin Bible School and serves as a children and youth counselor, investing deeply in the next generation. She has also completed church-planting training with DCPI (Dynamic Church Planting International).

Her ministry is marked by a love for young people, a commitment to discipleship, and a heart to see families and communities transformed by the power of the Gospel.
