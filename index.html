Our Calling

A Christ-centered ministry devoted to preserving sound doctrine, walking in holiness, and spreading the love of God to the world — rooted in Kaduna, Nigeria.

We exist to exalt Christ, disciple believers, and build a community marked by love and faithfulness.

Our Foundation

Built on Revelation & Faithfulness

Kingdom Philadelphia International is established upon the revelation of Jesus Christ — particularly the message to the Church in Philadelphia, commended for its faithfulness, obedience, and steadfastness.

We are committed to preserving truth and reflecting Christ's love to the world.
