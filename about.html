Who We Are

Our Identity

Kingdom Philadelphia International stands upon the revelation of Jesus Christ as revealed in the Book of Revelation, particularly the message to the Church in Philadelphia — commended for faithfulness, obedience, and steadfastness.

This divine commendation defines who we are. We are committed to:

  • check_circle Preserving sound doctrine
  • check_circle Walking in holiness
  • check_circle Responding to open doors set before us by Christ
  • check_circle Remaining faithful until the end
