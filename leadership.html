Lead Pastor & Visionary

Pastor Jamba Zira

Pastor Jamba Zira is the lead pastor of Kingdom Philadelphia International. With a heart devoted to truth, holiness, and love, he shepherds the ministry with spiritual clarity and unwavering commitment to the Word of God.

Under his leadership, the church continues to grow as a faithful body committed to reflecting Christ's authority and compassion to the world. With over a decade of devoted service, his anointed ministry has guided countless souls toward a deeper understanding of God's Kingdom.
